Frequently Asked Questions

What can I do in the GO Rentals App?

We're proud of our app and continue improving it to better serve you. The app currently lets you:

  • Update your profile (name, contact info, driver’s licence)
  • View all your booking details
  • Complete online Check-In
  • Request airport shuttle pickup
  • Make online bookings
  • Control features of the Tesla Model 3 or Model Y
If your question isn't answered here, please contact us!

How do I add an existing booking to the app?

If the email on your booking matches your app account email, it should show up in your app’s booking history automatically. If not, email us at Madicarrental@gmail.com to update the booking email.

Where can I download the app?

Search for ‘GO Rentals AU’ on the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). You can also find download links on our official website’s App page.

The app says I've been inactive — what should I do?

First, try signing out and logging back in. If that doesn’t help, check your app store for an update. Still having issues? Please contact us at Madicarrental@gmail.com.

Can I change my booking in the app?

Changes to vehicle bookings aren’t supported in the app yet. Please call us or email Madicarrental@gmail.com to change your vehicle or check upgrade availability.
