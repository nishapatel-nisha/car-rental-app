After your journey

Answering your questions about the end of your adventure and dropping off your vehicle

Frequently Asked Questions

If I return the car early, will I get a refund?

We understand that plans can change. Here's what happens:

Returning More Than 48 Hours Early:
You're eligible for a partial refund. We'll deduct 48 hours of rental charges + 10% of the remaining hire term. The rest will be refunded to your original card. A processing fee (greater of 10% or $20) applies.

Returning Less Than 48 Hours Early:
No refund is provided, as the vehicle has been reserved for your full term.

Online Cancellations:
If cancelled online with 48+ hours' notice, the same rules apply. Admin fee: 2% of the refund amount.

What if I’m running late and can’t return the car at the stated time on the contract?

You'll be charged an hourly rate for up to 4 hours. After that, a full day's rental and insurance will be charged.

What happens if I’m running late and return my car without a full tank of petrol?

We can refill it for you. You’ll be charged the fuel cost plus a possible $35 service fee depending on how much fuel is needed.

I've forgotten to pay a toll during my trip - what happens now?

If you opted for our $3/day Total Road Care package, tolls are covered. No need to worry. Refer to our toll guide for more info.

If I return my rental to your city offices, will your shuttle be able to take me to the airport or my accommodation?

Yes — for Auckland and Christchurch airports only, between 8am and 5pm, 7 days a week. Other branches are within walking distance of the terminals.

Do I have to clean my rental car before I drop it back off?

No need to fully clean it. Just remove rubbish and personal items. If the car is muddy, please rinse it off before returning.
