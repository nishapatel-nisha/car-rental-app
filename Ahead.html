Answering your questions about preparing for your trip and picking up your vehicle

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make changes to my reservation?

You can make changes to your reservation by calling our Australian office or emailing info@gorentals.com.au for support.

What will I need to bring when I collect my hire car?

Please bring a valid driver’s licence and a credit/debit card. A credit card is required if you selected the Basic insurance option. The card must have your name on it and be valid through your rental period.

If I pick up my vehicle out of hours, what is the pick-up process?

We’ll send you clear instructions and a damage check-sheet prior to your hire. Not all branches support after-hours pick-up — please call to confirm availability.

How do we arrange to get picked up by the airport shuttle once we arrive?

You can request an airport shuttle via the GO Rentals App or by calling +61 (add local number).

Can I change the vehicle on my booking after I have booked?

Yes, contact us to check availability and pricing for an upgrade. Call from Australia or email info@gorentals.com.au.

Can I add another driver to my booking when I come to pick up my rental?

Yes. Additional drivers must meet the licence requirements and must be present with their licence to be added to the Rental Agreement.

I'm staying in a downtown hotel — will the shuttle pick me up?

Currently, we do not offer hotel, Airbnb, or residential pickups from our city branches. Please call us for branch-specific shuttle info.

How long does it take to pick up and drop off a rental car with GO?

The process is quick, especially if you've done online check-in. If not, allow extra time to complete documents and payment at the branch.

Can I change the pick-up and drop-off location on my booking?

Yes. Contact us to check availability. Note that a one-way relocation fee may apply if locations differ.

If I cancel my hire after booking, what happens?

If cancelled more than 24 hours in advance, only a 10% fee applies. Within 24 hours: 100% cancellation fee. A refund admin fee applies to prepaid bookings.

Can I change my insurance cover when I pick up my car?

Yes. Our staff can assist with insurance upgrades when you collect the car. If you're arriving after-hours, changes must be made during online check-in.

How can I change my level of cover after I have booked?

Contact our Reservations Team — you’ll find all contact details on our website or confirmation email.

Do I have to choose pre-paid fuel at pick-up?

Yes, pre-paid fuel must be added during pick-up. Unfortunately, it cannot be added later once you’ve started your hire.

Is online check-in mandatory?

It's highly recommended and mandatory for after-hours pick-ups. It speeds up the process during regular hours as well.

Are my details saved for future vehicle reservations?

Online check-in does not save your details. Use the GO Rentals app to save your profile for future hires.

Do I still need to go to the counter?

Yes, for key collection and signing documents. If you checked in online, the process will be much faster.
